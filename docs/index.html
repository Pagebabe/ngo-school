About the project

Children’s Home of Education serves families in rural Cambodia where education is limited and many children spend their days working in fields or caring for animals. We offer free learning and a community space where children can grow with dignity.

Mission: Help the young Cambodian generation build a better future through education and opportunity.

Director’s story

Director Wei Ha grew up in poverty, spent 15 years as a monk, and learned multiple languages. He chose to share his knowledge with children who have the least access to education.

“Nobody gave me even one pencil.”

The school today

Free education, materials, and support for 160 children from seven nearby villages.

What we teach

  • English language
  • Morals and values
  • Cambodian history

Who we serve

  • Children aged 5–20
  • Families working in rice fields or construction
  • Communities without free schools nearby

What children receive

  • Free classes and materials
  • Books, pencils, and learning supplies
  • Food support when possible
